Welcome to the Data Track!

Using the tool is a three-step process:

First

Import your data from Google or Facebook

You can download your data from Google or Facebook. and upload it to Data Track. Then it is time to see what you have disclosed to them.

Second

Wait while the Data Track parses your data

Have you ever noticed you have bunch of data on Google and Facebook? To preserve your privacy your data is deleted from DataTrack database each time you close it

Third

Explore your data through the Data Track

Now it is the amazing time to explore your data through Data Track's different visualization modes
